我们是来自德国的独立游戏工作室Just2D Interactive。一年多前，我们推出了开放世界像素艺术RPG《困兽之国 Drova - Forsaken Kin》。这是我们的第一款正式作品，它融合了我们最爱的RPG元素，比如《哥特王朝1》、《上古卷轴3：晨风》和《巫师3》。在《困兽之国》里，我们刻意弱化了任务指引和地图标记等引导系统，以强调玩家自由探索。相较于多数现代RPG，这造就了它更具"复古"的游戏体验。您如何看待我们的设计理念？哪些元素令您欣赏或不悦？您最钟爱的RPG作品又是哪些？期待您的反馈！ We are Just2D Interactive, a small Indie game development company from Germany. Over a year ago we published Drova - Forsaken Kin, an Open World Pixel Art RPG and our first commercial product.It combines all the things we love about our favorite RPGs like Gothic 1 (probably not that known globally), Morrowind and The Witcher 3. We really wanted to emphasize on player autonomy, instead of guiding systems like quest markers and minimaps.This results in an "older"/more classical game experience compared to most modern RPGs.How do you like our design philosophy? What things do you like and/or don't like? And what are your favorite RPGs? Happy to hear from you!
