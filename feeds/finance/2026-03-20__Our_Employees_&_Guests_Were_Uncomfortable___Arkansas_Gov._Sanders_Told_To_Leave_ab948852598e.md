"Our Employees & Guests Were Uncomfortable": Arkansas Gov. Sanders Told To Leave Restaurant

            [*Authored by Jonathan Turley,*](https://jonathanturley.org/2026/03/20/our-employees-and-guests-were-uncomfortable-arkansas-gov-sanders-told-to-leave-arkansas-restaurant/)

**Republican Arkansas Gov. Sarah Huckabee Sanders was kicked out of another restaurant this week.** Years ago, I wrote about how Sanders, then the Trump White House spokesperson, was told [to leave the Red Hen restaurant in Lexington, Virginia](https://jonathanturley.org/2018/06/23/no-breakfast-for-you-red-hen-kicks-out-sarah-sanders-and-her-family/). Now, the [Croissanterie Restaurant](https://www.thecroissanterielr.com/) in Little Rock, Arkansas, has told the governor to leave because employees said they [felt uncomfortable](https://www.foxnews.com/politics/sarah-sanders-flipped-off-kicked-out-of-restaurant-that-claims-employees-were-uncomfortable) having her in the restaurant. One person yelled at her and flipped her off as she left with her friends and security.

Sanders went to the restaurant with three other moms for a quick meal. She recounted how she and the other moms were then told to leave:

***“Last week I was having lunch with two other moms at a restaurant when the owner approached a member of the State Police Executive Protection Detail and said my presence made their employees feel threatened and told us to leave.”***

[![](https://assets.zerohedge.com/s3fs-public/styles/inline_image_mobile/public/inline-images/Governor_Sarah_Huckabee_Sanders.jpg?itok=TjUQBoOV)](https://cms.zerohedge.com/s3/files/inline-images/Governor_Sarah_Huckabee_Sanders.jpg?itok=TjUQBoOV)

She added:*** ***

***“Arkansans are known for their warm hospitality, and while that restaurant certainly doesn’t meet that standard, my administration will continue to focus on lifting Arkansans up, not tearing others down with discrimination and hate.”***

Sanders had already started to eat when the restaurant’s owner approached a member of the security detail and requested that the governor leave.

The Croissanterie released a [lengthy statement](https://www.thv11.com/article/news/local/gov-sarah-huckabee-sanders-little-rock-restaurant-croissanterie/91-a6fecdc8-3dd3-41e9-b8b0-7800bad14fd2) and admitted that they told the governor and her party to leave. While offering a hand-ringing explanation about being “surprised and uncertain how best to respond,” it admitted that it “ultimately made the decision” to “support our employees and guests who expressed they were uncomfortable.”

It added, ***“We regret being placed in this position and having to make a difficult decision. However, we stand by our choice to support our employees and guests.”***

The restaurant is [founded and owned by Jill McDonald, executive chef, and Wendy Schay, pastry chef.](https://www.thecroissanterielr.com/about)

We have seen [various restaurants](https://jonathanturley.org/2016/12/28/serving-intolerance-with-an-anti-democratic-relish-hawaiian-restaurant-bars-trump-supporters/) refusing to serve Trump supporters,  conservatives, and [even those deemed allies](https://jonathanturley.org/2025/08/09/no-pierogi-for-you-dershowitz-denied-food-in-marthas-vineyard-over-his-political-views/). Democratic members of Congress have [defended such actions](https://jonathanturley.org/2018/06/23/no-breakfast-for-you-red-hen-kicks-out-sarah-sanders-and-her-family/) and even encouraged liberals to disrupt meals of conservatives.

Liberals [went to social media](https://www.facebook.com/OccupyDemocrats/posts/breaking-lol-maga-governor-sarah-huckabee-sanders-throws-a-massive-hissy-fit-aft/1461702759394627/) to celebrate the move by the restaurant. One posting from an employee declared:

***“Good Morning! [Sarah Huckabee Sanders](https://www.facebook.com/SarahHuckabeeSanders?__cft__%5b0%5d=AZZt9d28PiHQj4SK0QTOM6D6g2_d0LEz6NSkKzFrYq4oLwxTcrHeifArrnNzI50GeIxy_u_89JXfyTGK3XzPbKOkwQpZTS02RsoQ92w6RpBzQNyTwp-4Qp_lfFpRy9pqV925HMrUCdHYtCIWEbo4vpAHuzLzHtSGMFHsokNQ-Sw4Fg&__tn__=-%5dK-R) no amount of evil you send our way can ever take our smiles away!!! I’m proud af to work here! I’m proud af to be gay and I’m proud af to be an Arkansan. My voice matters. Try again.”***
