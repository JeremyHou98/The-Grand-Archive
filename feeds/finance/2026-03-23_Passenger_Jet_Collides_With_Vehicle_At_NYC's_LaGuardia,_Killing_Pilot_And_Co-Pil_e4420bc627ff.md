Passenger Jet Collides With Vehicle At NYC's LaGuardia, Killing Pilot And Co-Pilot

An Air Canada Express CRJ-900 regional jet operated by Jazz Aviation collided with a fire truck while landing at New York's LaGuardia Airport (LGA) late Sunday night at 23:40 ET, instantly killing the pilot and co-pilot. There were approximately 76 people on board.

> 
> — BNO News (@BNONews) [March 23, 2026](https://twitter.com/BNONews/status/2035933231015489615?ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw)

The flight-tracking website [FlightRadar24](https://x.com/flightradar24/status/2035998230727549369?s=20) said data show Flight AC8646 from Montreal landed on Runway 4 and was rolling down the runway when it struck the fire truck.

[![](https://assets.zerohedge.com/s3fs-public/styles/inline_image_mobile/public/inline-images/HEE1u8lW0AAs6pa.png?itok=Uq1IU3P0)](https://cms.zerohedge.com/s3/files/inline-images/HEE1u8lW0AAs6pa.png?itok=Uq1IU3P0)

FlightRadar24 shows that Flight AC8646's groundspeed was around 24 mph at the time of the incident.

[![](https://assets.zerohedge.com/s3fs-public/styles/inline_image_mobile/public/inline-images/HEEWQvmXcAAJpqm.jpg?itok=e5gsAtlj)](https://cms.zerohedge.com/s3/files/inline-images/HEEWQvmXcAAJpqm.jpg?itok=e5gsAtlj)

Early Monday, Kathryn Garcia, executive director of the Port Authority of New York and New Jersey, told reporters that 41 passengers and crew members were taken to the hospital, with 32 later released. She said some passengers sustained very serious injuries.
