An oil supertanker hauling two million barrels of Iraq’s crude has got through the Strait of Hormuz, according to tanker-tracking data compiled by Bloomberg, the first vessel observed moving Baghdad’s barrels through the vital waterway since it all but closed to commercial shipping at the start of the war.
