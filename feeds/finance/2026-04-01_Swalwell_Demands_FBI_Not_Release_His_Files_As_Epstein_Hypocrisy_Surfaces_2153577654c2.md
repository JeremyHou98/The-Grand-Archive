Swalwell Demands FBI Not Release His Files As Epstein Hypocrisy Surfaces

_Authored by [Luis Cornelio](https://headlineusa.com/author/lcornelio) via [Headline USA](https://headlineusa.com/subscribe/),_

Embattled Rep. [Eric Swalwell](https://headlineusa.com/tag/Eric-Swalwell), D-Calif., who has long demanded that President [Donald Trump](https://headlineusa.com/tag/Donald-Trump) release the Epstein files, now faces scrutiny as the [FBI](https://headlineusa.com/tag/FBI) prepares to release documents tied to its investigation into his alleged ties to a Chinese intelligence operative.

[![](https://assets.zerohedge.com/s3fs-public/styles/inline_image_mobile/public/inline-images/800-7-e1654636255949-696x350.jpg?itok=TcMK36OT)](https://cms.zerohedge.com/s3/files/inline-images/800-7-e1654636255949-696x350.jpg?itok=TcMK36OT)

The scrutiny comes after Swalwell demanded Monday that FBI Director [Kash Patel](https://headlineusa.com/tag/Kash-Patel) refrain from **declassifying the potentially damaging files, which could shed light on his relationship with Christine Fang, the suspected Chinese spy also known as “Fang Fang.”**

On X, critics highlighted what they called blatant hypocrisy, pointing to Swalwell’s prior demands that Trump release the Epstein files.

One widely shared X post from July 2025 [read](https://x.com/RepSwalwell/status/1948090948237554002?s=20), _**“Trump has the files. Why won’t HE release them,” in reference to the Epstein files.**_

On Sept. 25, Swalwell himself replied to a post from Patel, [writing](https://x.com/RepSwalwell/status/1971374202306560005?s=20), _**“Blah blah blah. Where are the Epstein Files?”**_

On Oct. 7, Swalwell again [pressed](https://x.com/RepSwalwell/status/1975738986485403668?s=20) Patel, adding, _**“Where are the Epstein Files?”**_

Just 10 days later, Swalwell [escalated](https://twitter.com/RepSwalwell/status/1979280375995326727?s=20) further, _**branding House Speaker Mike Johnson the “pro-pedophile Speaker” and again demanding the Epstein files be released.**_

Swalwell conveyed his opposition in a cease-and-desist letter to Patel, urging that his own FBI files are not released.

_**“The congressman has never been accused of wrongdoing in that matter and your attempt to release the file is a transparent attempt to smear him and undermine his campaign for governor of California,”**_ the letter said, as quoted by the leftist _Washington Post_.

It added, “Your actions threaten to expose you, others at the FBI, and the FBI itself to significant legal liability.”

The _Post_ reported over the weekend that Patel had deployed agents to California to review the documents ahead of a potential public release.

**The forthcoming release of the files could provide clarity on the FBI’s investigation into Swalwell’s early political career.** He joined Congress in 2013 after serving three years on the Dublin City Council.

The probe centered on Fang’s alleged efforts to act as a Chinese intelligence operative by cultivating relationships with up-and-coming politicians.

**Swalwell has not definitively denied having a sexual relationship with Fang. He purportedly cut off contact with her after being briefed by the FBI.**

What Swalwell knew about Fang, and what he told the FBI, has largely remained speculative, but that could change if the files were released.
