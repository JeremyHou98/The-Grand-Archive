President Trump says the U.S. would postpone strikes on Iran’s energy infrastructure after “productive conversations” with Tehran. Iranian officials deny any negotiations, calling the claims a tactic to lower global energy prices. Mehran Kamrava, professor at Georgetown University in Qatar, told Joumanna Bercetche on Horizons Middle East and Africa that the war has been marked by miscalculations on both sides.
