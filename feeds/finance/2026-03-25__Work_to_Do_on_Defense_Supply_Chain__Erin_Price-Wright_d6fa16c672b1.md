Erin Price-Wright, general partner at Andreessen Horowitz, joins Dani Burger on "Bloomberg Deals." They discuss defense tech investments as the Iran War reveals gaps in spending. The Pentagon wants to shift roughly $1.5 billion in previously approved funding to buy critical missile interceptors from Lockheed Martin and RTX, according to the acting comptroller — weapons that are in short supply as the war in Iran consumes vast amounts of the munitions.
