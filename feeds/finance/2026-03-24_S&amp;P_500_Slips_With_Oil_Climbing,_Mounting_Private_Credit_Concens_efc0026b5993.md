US stocks opened in the red as the price of oil rebounded, with fighting between the US-Israel alliance and Iran raging unabated. Alternative asset managers also declined after Ares Management Corp. and Apollo Global Management Inc. became the latest firms to curb withdrawals from some funds, rekindling investor concerns about private credit.
