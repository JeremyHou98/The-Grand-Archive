**Florida’s beach towns are getting stuck playing spring break whack-a-mole as rowdy crowds organize chaotic “takeovers” on social media,** unleashing booze-fueled flash mobs that turn sunny shores into scenes of violence and mass arrests, according to the [New York Post](https://nypost.com/2026/03/24/us-news/how-spring-breakers-turn-florida-beaches-into-war-zones/).
