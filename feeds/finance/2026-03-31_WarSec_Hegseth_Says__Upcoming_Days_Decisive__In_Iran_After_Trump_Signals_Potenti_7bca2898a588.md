## Off-Ramp Imminent? Trump Tells World "Go Get Your Own Oil" Via Strait After 'Decimating' Iran

There's been a lot of speculation that the White House is preparing to find a 'mission accomplished' declaration moment, as 'any offramp will do' as a way to avoid a costly potential quagmire of introducing ground troops, and we may be seeing the start of one.

After comments apparently leaked to [The Wall Street Journal](https://www.wsj.com/world/middle-east/trump-iran-war-strait-of-hormuz-ee950ad4?mod=WSJ_home_mediumtopper_pos_1) overnight that **Trump is willing to leave Iran with the Strait unopened**, the President has clarified his thinking in his out loud voice this morning.

[![](https://assets.zerohedge.com/s3fs-public/styles/inline_image_mobile/public/inline-images/p0n5w0my.jpg?itok=VBNRaUHh)](https://cms.zerohedge.com/s3/files/inline-images/p0n5w0my.jpg?itok=VBNRaUHh)

President Trump has posted on social media this morning, clearly signaling he is further down the road towards an off-ramp:

> _**All of those countries that can’t get jet fuel because of the Strait of Hormuz,** like the United Kingdom, which refused to get involved in the decapitation of Iran, I have a suggestion for you:_
> 
> _Number 1, buy from the U.S., we have plenty, and_
> 
> _Number 2, build up some delayed courage, go to the Strait, and just TAKE IT._
> 
> _You’ll have to start learning how to fight for yourself, the U.S.A. won’t be there to help you anymore, just like you weren’t there for us._
> 
> _**Iran has been, essentially, decimated**._
> 
> **_The hard part is done. Go get your own oil!_**
> 
> _President DJT_
