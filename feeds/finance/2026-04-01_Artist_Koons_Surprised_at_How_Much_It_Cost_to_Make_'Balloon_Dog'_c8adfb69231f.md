American artist Jeff Koons talks about his iconic Balloon Dog sculptures and the costs that come with making them. He discusses his process, the scale of the work, and the investment required to produce these pieces. Koons appears on this week's episode of The David Rubenstein Show: Peer to Peer Conversations. This interview was recorded February 2 in New York. (Source: Bloomberg)
