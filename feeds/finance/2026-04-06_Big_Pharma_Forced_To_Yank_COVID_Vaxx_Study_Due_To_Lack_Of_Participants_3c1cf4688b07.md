Big Pharma Forced To Yank COVID Vaxx Study Due To Lack Of Participants

_Authored by [Ben Sellers](https://headlineusa.com/author/bensellers) via [Headline USA](https://headlineusa.com/subscribe/),_

**Two of the major [pharmaceutical companies](https://headlineusa.com/tag/big-pharma) connected with the controversial [COVID vaccines](https://headlineusa.com/tag/vaccines) were forced to abandon a new research study after failing to garner enough participants.**

[![](https://assets.zerohedge.com/s3fs-public/styles/inline_image_mobile/public/inline-images/AP21144719813912-scaled-e1625783.jpg?itok=fHgUuoA4)](https://cms.zerohedge.com/s3/files/inline-images/AP21144719813912-scaled-e1625783.jpg?itok=fHgUuoA4)

[Pfizer](https://headlineusa.com/tag/pfizer) and German vax maker BioNTech had **sought to research an updated version of the vaccine in adults ages 50 to 64, but were unable to generate the data needed due to the low enrollment** in the trials, [Reuters reported](https://www.reuters.com/business/healthcare-pharmaceuticals/pfizer-biontech-halt-us-covid-vaccine-study-after-recruitment-struggles-2026-04-01/).

The study was needed in order to meet new guidelines imposed by the [Food and Drug Administration](https://headlineusa.com/tag/food-and-drug-administration) that require the pharmaceutical companies to provide data on the efficacy of the vaccine in comparison with a placebo.

However, it marks a peculiar coda to the pandemic era, when [mass formation psychosis](https://headlineusa.com/tag/mass-formation-psychosis) swept the globe forcing individuals to forgo their civil liberties _en masse_ and to inject the experimental, gene-altering serum into their DNA under extreme social duress.
